About
This website was created in the Spring 2022 Semester for CS-545 : Human Computer Interaction. The course covers basic concepts, principles, and frameworks in HCI; models of interaction; and design guidelines and methodologies. The course includes extensive readings and reports, as well as work on projects involving interface design and development. Students are required to collaborate on a group project in which these concepts are implemented.
